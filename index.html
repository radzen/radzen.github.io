Los Angeles, CA

Certified in computer technology, Randy has been tinkering with computers for as long as he can remember. He coded his first program in Pascal then he learned Visual Basic and a little bit of C while in college.

His insatiable curiosity towards technology and fondness for stunning graphics led him to start a long journey to the world of web development. Armed with his Google-fu and a fast internet connection, he learned HTML, CSS and Javascript.
